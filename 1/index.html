Palazzo Versace Rooms & Suites

Steeped in classic Italian design, each of the hotel's 215 luxury accommodations reflect the true essence of the Versace brand.
Here, ‘luxury’ lies in the material – parquet flooring, a canvas of elaborate white and cream boiseries,
and a pastel palette of silk furnishings.
